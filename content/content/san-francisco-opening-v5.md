OPENING WEEK: DECEMBER 15-21

## Seven Days, Seven Experiences

**Monday Dec 15: Grand Opening Kickoff**\
Join Mayor London Breed for the ribbon cutting at 10am. All-day celebration with 20% off storewide. Evening reception features local craft beer from Fort Point, wine from Napa, and bites from Tartine. Live DJ set 6-9pm. RSVP for evening event.

**Tuesday Dec 16: Run Club Launch**\
6:30am: Inaugural WKND SF Run Club departs for Embarcadero route (5 miles). All paces welcome. Post-run coffee and pastries. Free running gear giveaway. Meet your new running crew.

**Wednesday Dec 17: Sustainable Style Workshop**\
7pm: "Building a Capsule Wardrobe for SF Living." Learn to create 30+ outfits from 15 versatile pieces that handle our weather. Complimentary personal styling session included. Limited to 30 participants.

**Thursday Dec 18: Bike to Shop Day**\
Ride your bike, get 15% off. Secure bike parking. Free basic bike maintenance clinic 12-2pm. Meet local cycling advocates. Helmet decorating station. Kids welcome.

**Friday Dec 19: Surf & Style Panel**\
7pm: Panel discussion featuring local surfers and sustainable fashion experts. "From Ocean Beach to Office: The SF Lifestyle." Q&A, networking, refreshments. Free admission.

**Saturday Dec 20: Community Day**\
10am-6pm: 10% of all sales benefit SF-Marin Food Bank. Free alterations all day. Kids' eco-craft corner. DJ spinning vinyl. Raffle for $500 shopping spree. Photo booth with Golden Gate backdrop.

**Sunday Dec 21: Trail to Tavern Social**\
11am: Group hike departs for Lands End Trail (moderate, 3 miles). Return to store for closing celebration toast at 2pm. Share your adventure stories. Final day of opening specials.
