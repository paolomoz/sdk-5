SUSTAINABLE STYLE, CALIFORNIA SPIRIT

## Built for How You Live

San Francisco moves differently. Early morning runs through Golden Gate Park. Bike commutes across the bridge. Weekend adventures in Marin. Evening gatherings in the Mission. Our store celebrates this rhythm with collections designed for the active, conscious Bay Area lifestyle.

Reclaimed materials from local sources. Energy systems powered by renewable sources. A community space for workshops, panels, and neighborhood gatherings. This is retail reimagined for San Francisco values—where every purchase supports sustainable practices and local artisans.

OPENING WEEK EXPERIENCES

## December 15-21: Seven Days of Discovery

**Monday, December 15: Grand Opening**\
10am: Official ribbon cutting. All-day: 20% off storewide. 6pm-9pm: Opening night celebration with local craft beer from Almanac, wine from Napa's sustainable vineyards, and bites from Tartine Bakery. DJ set by Bay Area artists. RSVP required for evening.

**Tuesday, December 16: Morning Run Club**\
6:30am: Inaugural WKND Run Club departs for Embarcadero waterfront route (5 miles, all paces). Post-run coffee from Ritual Roasters and pastries from Arizmendi. Free running buffs for first 50 participants. Build your running community.

**Wednesday, December 17: Layering Masterclass**\
7pm: "The San Francisco Capsule Wardrobe." Learn to layer like a local—15 pieces that create 40+ outfit combinations. Handle our microclimates with style. Complimentary 15-minute styling consultation. Limited to 30 attendees.

**Thursday, December 18: Pedal & Shop**\
All day: Arrive by bike, receive 15% off your purchase. 12pm-2pm: Free basic bike tune-ups from Box Dog Bikes. Meet advocacy groups working to make SF more bike-friendly. Helmet decorating station. Secure bike parking all day.

**Friday, December 19: Beach Culture Panel**\
7pm: "From Ocean Beach to Market Street: SF Surf Style." Panel with local surfers, sustainable fashion advocates, and coastal conservation leaders. Q&A, drinks, good conversation. Free admission, RSVP recommended.

**Saturday, December 20: Community Day**\
10am-6pm: 15% of all sales benefit SF-Marin Food Bank. Clothing drive for One Warm Coat. Free basic alterations all day. Kids' corner with eco-friendly art projects. Live music. Photo booth with GG Bridge backdrop. Grand prize drawing at 5pm.

**Sunday, December 21: Trails to Ales**\
10am: Group hike to Lands End with coastal views (3 miles, moderate, all welcome). 1pm: Return to store for closing celebration. Share trail stories over local beer and cider. Last chance for opening week specials. Thank you toast at 2pm.

WHY WKND SAN FRANCISCO

## More Than a Store

**Microclimate Experts** – Our staff lives here. They know fog, wind, and sun. Get personalized layering advice that actually works.

**Transit Friendly** – MUNI, BART, bike, or walk. We're steps from Van Ness Station with secure bike parking and shower facilities.

**Repair, Don't Replace** – On-site alterations and garment repair services. Keep your favorite pieces in rotation longer.

**Community First** – Weekly run clubs, monthly bike rides, quarterly trail adventures. Join the WKND SF crew.

**Local Makers** – Partnerships with 12 Bay Area designers and artisans. Exclusive pieces made in the Bay.

**Sustainable Always** – 100% of our inventory meets strict environmental standards. Quality over quantity, every time.
